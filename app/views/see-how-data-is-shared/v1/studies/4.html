How your data has been used

Data on mortality in the British Regional Heart Study is used for research focusing on understanding ways to prevent mortality from diseases such as cardiovascular disease.

The study aims are to investigate patterns in mortality of chronic diseases over time as well as to identify factors that predispose to greater risk of mortality in later life.

Detailed follow-up of information on mortality is essential for these research aims. For example, previous research has demonstrated a decline in mortality from heart disease in Britain.

The goal of this research is to inform change in policy and clinical practice to prevent deaths from chronic diseases.
