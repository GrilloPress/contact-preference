How your data has been used

The primary output is an online intelligence platform available to users containing over 500 benchmarked metrics on NHS activity. This is designed to be visually attractive to users but convey rich statistical intelligence.

The platform includes domain dashboards, filtered views with users able to explore areas of interest and shape metric views to their requirements, users are able to create bespoke reports containing information relevant to them.
