How do you get a new one?

You can get an NHS COVID Pass through:

Testing

Getting a negative LFT or PCR test through a test site (where reporting is automatic,) or you can self-report using the 'Register my test result' service. Your new NHS COVID Pass will be valid for 72 hours.

Vaccination

Once two weeks have passed from your second vaccine you will be able to get a NHS COVID Pass.
