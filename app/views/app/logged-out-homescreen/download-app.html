Use NHS App online to access services on your desktop or laptop computer, through your web browser. You do not need to install anything to get started.

Use this service to:

  • get your NHS COVID Pass
  • order repeat prescriptions
  • book and manage appointments
  • get health information and advice
  • view your health record securely
  • view your NHS number

Before you start

To use this service you must be:

  • registered with a GP surgery in England
  • aged 13 and over
