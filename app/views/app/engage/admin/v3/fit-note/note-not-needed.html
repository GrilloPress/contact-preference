Request a fit note

You do not need a fit note

If you are off work sick for 7 days or less, you do not need to give your employer a sick note or other medical evidence.

Instead, you may be asked to confirm that you have been ill. Your employer will agree on how you should do this. They may ask you to fill in a form. This is called self-certification.

You can also self-certify your illness by completing the statement of sickness form on the HMRC website.
