Data from your health records can be used for research and planning in the NHS.

Data helps the NHS to:

  • plan and improve health and care services
  • research and develop cures for serious illnesses

Becoming a data donor

You can choose to help the NHS by joining the data donor list and taking part in new research studies.

If you do become a donor we'll match you with research studies and ask you if you're happy to take part.

If you agree to take part we will then share your details and data with the organisation running the study.

Joining the register will not change your individual care.
